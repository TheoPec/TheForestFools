Patch 1.0.1 — Terrain, Ports & QoL

New features:
- Logo fix: "THE FOREST FOOLS" now displays correctly, version shown at launch.
- Colored terrain: the map now has water (blue) on borders, sand (yellow) on
  coasts and grass (green/dark green) inland. Water is impassable.
- Ports: new location on sand tiles with dock (chalutier, future update), tavern
  and merchant.
- sleep command: sleep at a tavern for 10 gold — fully restores HP, grants some
  XP and resets merchant stock.
- unequip command: unequip weapon or armor.
- Mob respawn: enemies and loot respawn after 15 actions.
- Grouped inventory: duplicates are displayed with a count (x3).
- Keyboard shortcuts: h, m, l, t, s, f, i, e, u, b, q.
- Auto-completion: typing the start of an item name is enough
  (e.g. "sell sm" → Small Potion).

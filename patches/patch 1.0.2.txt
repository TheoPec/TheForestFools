Patch 1.0.2 — Magic, Dodge & Exploration

New features:
- Mana: new mana system (50 base, +10 per level-up, restored by sleeping at a
  tavern).
- Spells: buy spells from the Mage (castle/capital courtyard) or the Warlock
  (castle tower). Max 3 spells. Use "cast" in combat to cast them.
- Full map: buy a map from the Explorer at the port dock (200 gold) to reveal
  all locations at once.
- Dodge: armors now have a dodge % (e.g. Shadow Cloak 20%, Leather Vest 10%).
  Each enemy attack can be dodged.
- Defense: "Defense" stat replaces "Armor" in the display.
- cheat command: type "cheat" to unlock everything (level 20, 99999 gold, all
  items/spells, full map). For beta testing.

Bug fixes:
- NPC limit: NPCs now only reveal up to 2 locations. After that, buy the map.
- Dragon nerf: the dragon now has 350 HP and 40 ATK (no more scaling).

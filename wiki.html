Category: World

    

      

        
Subcategory: Map

        
    
          
  • world generated on a 10x10 grid, columns A to J and rows 1 to 10
    • 
          
  • the capital is always placed at E5
    • 
          
  • other locations are placed randomly each game
    • 
          
  • hidden locations remain unrevealed until a dialogue or event reveals them
    • 
        

      


      

        
Subcategory: Location Distribution

        
    
          
  • forest x3
    • 
          
  • village x3
    • 
          
  • cave x2
    • 
          
  • castle x2
    • 
          
  • dungeon x2
    • 
          
  • port x? (on sand tiles)
    • 
          
  • dragon_lair x1
    • 
          
  • the rest of the grid is composed of plains
    • 
        

      


      

        
Subcategory: Terrain

        
    
          
  • water (blue) — borders of the map, impassable
    • 
          
  • sand (yellow) — coastal tiles between water and land
    • 
          
  • grass (green / dark green) — interior of the map
    • 
        

        
added in patch 1.0.1

      


      

        
Subcategory: Map Symbols

        
    
          
  • . = plains
    • 
          
  • T = forest
    • 
          
  • V = village
    • 
          
  • O = cave
    • 
          
  • # = castle
    • 
          
  • X = dungeon
    • 
          
  • K = capital
    • 
          
  • D = dragon_lair
    • 
          
  • P = port
    • 
          
  • ~ = water (impassable)
    • 
          
  • : = sand

Category: Quests

    

      

        
Subcategory: Main Quest

        
Name
slay the ancient dragon

        
Quest Giver
The King in capital > throne_room

        
Starting Condition
reach level 5 then speak to the king

        
Effect
the king reveals the Dragon Lair position on the map

        
Objective
go to the nest in Dragon Lair and kill The Ancient Dragon

      


      

        
Subcategory: Quest States

        
    
          
  • none : no quest received
    • 
          
  • given : quest given by the king, dragon still alive
    • 
          
  • completed : dragon slain, kingdom saved
    • 
        

        
king's final dialogue: the player is remembered as a legend.

Category: Systems

    

      

        
Subcategory: Player

        
    
          
  • start: 100 HP, 50 Mana, 20 gold, level 1
    • 
          
  • base attack: 3
    • 
          
  • base defense: 0
    • 
          
  • starting equipment: Rusty Sword and Cloth Rags
    • 
          
  • starting inventory: Small Potion
    • 
          
  • starting position: E5
    • 
        

      


      

        
Subcategory: Progression

        
    
          
  • initial XP required: 50
    • 
          
  • each level: +10 max HP, +10 max Mana, full heal, +1 base attack
    • 
          
  • required XP is then multiplied by 1.5
    • 
          
  • standard enemies scale up with player level
    • 
          
  • the dragon has fixed stats (350 HP, 40 ATK)
    • 
        

      


      

        
Subcategory: Combat

        
    
          
  • combat zones: dungeon, cave, forest, castle
    • 
          
  • actions: attack, cast (spell), use potion, flee
    • 
          
  • damage received is reduced by defense via raw damage - defense // 2
    • 
          
  • dodge: each enemy attack can be dodged based on armor dodge % (e.g. Shadow Cloak 20%)
    • 
          
  • successful flee: 50% chance
    • 
          
  • a zone can be temporarily marked cleared after victory
    • 
          
  • enemies and loot respawn after 15 actions
    • 
        

      


      

        
Subcategory: Mana & Spells

        
    
          
  • base mana: 50, +10 per level-up
    • 
          
  • mana is restored by sleeping at a tavern (10 gold)
    • 
          
  • buy spells from the Mage (castle/capital courtyard) or the Warlock (castle tower)
    • 
          
  • maximum of 3 spells equipped at a time
    • 
          
  • use "cast" command during combat to cast a spell
    • 
        

        
added in patch 1.0.2

      


      

        
Subcategory: Sleep

        
    
          
  • available at any tavern (village or port)
    • 
          
  • costs 10 gold
    • 
          
  • fully restores HP and Mana
    • 
          
  • gives a small amount of XP
    • 
          
  • resets merchant stock
    • 
        

        
added in patch 1.0.1

      


      

        
Subcategory: Loot And Search

        
    
          
  • the search command only works inside a location
    • 
          
  • major search zones: treasure_room, crypt, altar, ruins, clearing, throne_room, great_hall, tower, dungeon_cells, hoard
    • 
          
  • search gives gold and may give an item
    • 
          
  • fights can also drop consumables, misc items, weapons or armor
    • 
          
  • an empty location cannot be searched a second time
    • 
        

      


      

        
Subcategory: Merchant

        
    
          
  • stock generated randomly
    • 
          
  • up to 3 different consumables
    • 
          
  • between 1 and 3 weapons
    • 
          
  • 60% chance to add an armor
    • 
          
  • items resold at half price, minimum 1 gold
    • 
        

      


      

        
Subcategory: Commands

        
    
          
  • help (h)
    • 
          
  • map (m)
    • 
          
  • cd {cell}, cd {location}, cd {subloc}, cd ..
    • 
          
  • look (l)
    • 
          
  • talk (t)
    • 
          
  • stats (s)
    • 
          
  • fight (f)
    • 
          
  • inventory / inv (i)
    • 
          
  • equip {item} (e)
    • 
          
  • unequip {weapon|armor} (u)
    • 
          
  • use {item}
    • 
          
  • buy {item} (b)
    • 
          
  • sell {item}
    • 
          
  • cast {spell} — during combat
    • 
          
  • sleep — at tavern, costs 10 gold
    • 
          
  • search
    • 
          
  • cheat — unlocks everything (beta testing)
    • 
          
  • quit / exit (q)
    • 
        

        
auto-completion: type the beginning of an item name (e.g. "sell sm" → Small Potion).

        
keyboard shortcuts in parentheses, added in patch 1.0.1.

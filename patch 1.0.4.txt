Patch 1.0.4 — Zone Enemies, Level Gating & Castle Treasure

Zone-based enemies:
- Enemies are now specific to each zone type, with increasing difficulty:
  * Forest (level 1+): Giant Rat, Wild Crow, Plague Rat Swarm, Feral Boar,
    Giant Spider, Goblin Scavenger, Venomous Snake.
  * Cave (level 3+): Cave Wolf, Brown Bear, Cave Bat Swarm, Rock Troll,
    Shadow Stalker, Undead Miner.
  * Castle (level 6+): Living Armor, Haunted Knight, Hollow Wraith,
    Cursed Sentinel, Spectral Guard, Gargoyle.
  * Dungeon (level 9+): Cursed Knight, Tomb Guardian, Dark Sorcerer,
    Bone Colossus, Undead Priest, Abyssal Demon.
- Each zone's enemies still scale with player level on top of their base stats.

NPC level gating:
- NPCs now only hint about locations the player is strong enough to handle.
  Forests are mentioned from level 1, caves from level 3, castles from
  level 6, and dungeons from level 9.
- When there are harder zones the player can't access yet, NPCs say:
  "I know of darker places... but you haven't the stature for them."
  with the required level shown.
- NPC POI assignments refresh when the player reaches a new zone tier,
  so new locations become available as the player levels up.

Old Key — Castle Treasure:
- Castles now have a treasure_room sub-location.
- The treasure_room contains a locked iron-bound chest that requires an
  Old Key to open.
- Opening the chest gives generous loot: 80-200 gold, a powerful weapon
  or armor (Steel Sword, War Hammer, Dark Blade, Plate Armor, Dark Plate
  or Silver Longsword), and a bonus consumable.
- The Old Key is consumed after use (crumbles to dust).
- Each castle's chest can only be opened once.
- If the player has no Old Key, a message tells them they need one.
